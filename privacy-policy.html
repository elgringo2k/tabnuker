Tab Nuker — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What data we collect

Tab Nuker stores only the list of domain names you choose to add. Nothing else is collected, recorded, or transmitted.

How data is stored

Your domain list is saved using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.sync API. This means the list syncs across your Chrome profiles via your Google account, the same way Chrome syncs bookmarks and settings. Tab Nuker itself never sends your data anywhere.

What data we do NOT collect

  • No browsing history
  • No tab URLs beyond what is needed in the moment to check for a domain match
  • No personal information of any kind
  • No analytics or crash reports

Third parties

Tab Nuker does not share data with any third party. There are no analytics SDKs, ad networks, or external services of any kind.
